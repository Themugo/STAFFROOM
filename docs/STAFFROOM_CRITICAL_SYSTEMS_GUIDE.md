**Solution**: AI-powered resignation risk scoring (0-100%) with leading indicators.

**What it shows:**
- Who's likely to leave (ranked by risk score)
- When they'll likely leave
- Why they're at risk (engagement, performance, pay gaps, tenure)
- Cost impact if they leave
- Immediate intervention recommendations

**Real data example:**
```

├─ Engagement: 62% (down from 85% expected)
├─ Absences: 8 days/month (up 300% from normal)
├─ Performance: 79% (down from 85%)

```

**Business Impact:**
- Identify 3-5 at-risk people before they leave
- Cost to retain: $5-10k engagement intervention
- Cost to replace: $180k salary + benefits + hiring + onboarding + lost productivity
- ROI: Prevent 1 departure = Save $150k+

#### **2. 💪 EMPLOYEE HEALTH SCORE** (WELLNESS & PERFORMANCE)
**Problem**: Can't see who's struggling until they quit, get sick, or burnout.  
**Solution**: Real-time health score (0-100%) combining performance, engagement, wellness.

**What it shows:**
- Overall health score (composite metric)
- Performance trend (is it improving/declining?)
- Engagement level (sentiment/morale)
- Burnout risk (workload analysis)
- Promotion readiness
- Top performers vs people needing support

**Health score formula:**
```
Base: 100%
- If performance < 70%: -20%
- If engagement < 60%: -25%
- If absences > 6/month: -15%
- If risk score > 50%: -20%
= Final health score
```

**Real data example:**
```
Peter Otieno: 90% health ✓ STAR
├─ Performance: 91%
├─ Engagement: 84%
├─ Burnout: Low (2 absences)
└─ Ready for: Senior Developer (88% ready)

James Kamau: 45% health ⚠️ AT RISK
├─ Performance: 79% (declining)
├─ Engagement: 62% (concerning)
├─ Burnout: High (8 absences)
└─ Action: Immediate support needed
```

**Business Impact:**
- Identify burnout before it happens
- Catch performance issues early
- See who's ready for promotion
- Prevent unnecessary departures from stress

## 📊 AI INTELLIGENCE POWERING EACH SYSTEM

### **Resignation Risk Scoring** (Predictive ML Model)
Factors analyzed:
- Engagement trend (↓15%+ = risk signal)
- Performance decline (↓10+ points = risk)
- Absence increase (↑50% = risk)
- Tenure risk (4-5 year itch window)
- Compensation gap (below market = risk)
- Manager change (transition risk)
- Promotion delays (2+ times)
- External signals (if tracked)

**Risk Score = Weighted combination of all factors**

---

### **Health Score** (Composite Wellness Model)
Combines:
- Performance metrics (technical output)
- Engagement/morale (sentiment)
- Work-life balance (absences, burnout)
- Retention risk (see above)

**Produces holistic view of person's wellbeing**

### **Skills Analysis** (Supply/Demand Matching)
Detects:
